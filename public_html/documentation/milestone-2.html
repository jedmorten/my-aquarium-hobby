Content Strategy

At the top of my site, I will have a navbar that will navigate to specific areas of the page since the page might end up being long.

Under the navbar there will be a picture of my aquarium that will cover the entire width of the page. It will also display a welcome message.

Under the welcome section, there will be two picture carousels. The left carousel will display images of my fish. The right will display images of my live plants.

Under the two carousels, there will be a text section where I describe how long I've been keeping aquariums, and the upgrades in equipment that I've made along the way.

Next will be a gallery of photos that illustrate the journey section. The images will start with the first aquarium that I had, then the second, then the third.

After the Journey gallery, I will use a carousel of cards that will show the equipment that I use in my current aquarium setup. Each card will have an image cap of the equipment piece, a small description of what it does, and a link where to buy it.

The next section will be a container with two boxes. The left box will contain a contact form, and the right box will contain an about me section.

Lastly, there will be a footer at the bottom of the page with some sort of parting information in it.
