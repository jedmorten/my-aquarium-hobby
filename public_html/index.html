My Fish and Plants

The Journey

I began my aquarium hobby journey in 2013. After getting hooked on the TV show "Tanked", I bought a 10 gallon aquarium kit and stocked it with some neon tetras and a few plants. I soon realized that based on my wants, a 10 gallon tank was not going to be enough. Later that year I upgraded to a 30 gallon aquarium kit and stocked it with more fish and plants. While the 30 gallon aquarium did give me more space to try some bigger fish and more plants, I again outgrew it quickly.

In 2015, I upgraded the size of my tank again. This time I doubled the size and got a 60 gallon aquarium. This aquarium was where most of my experience was built. I was finally able to have multiple schools of different fish species, and started to research how to better care for aquatic plants. This tank is where I had my biggest challenges, then my biggest successes after I worked through them. After five years in the 60 gallon tank it had become overcrowded by plants. It was time to upgrade again. This time I researched and bought all the equipment separately instead of buying another kit, so I could get my ideal setup. I upgraded to a 125 gallon aquarium in 2020, which is what I still have today. The gallery below contains all my aquariums that I've had along the way.

About Me

I had an aquarium as a kid, and always had fond memories of it. For some reason, I also remember it being bigger than it actually was. I purchased my first aquarium as an adult nine years ago, and I've had one ever since.

This is the first website I have ever created, and was created as part of the Deep Dive Coding Bootcamp at CNM Ingenuity. It was created as a way to showcase the skills that I have learned and will continue to improve on.
